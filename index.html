Hi! My name is Peihang Wu (吴沛航). I am currently a postdoc at Beijing International Center for Mathematical Research (BICMR), Peking University. Before coming to BICMR, I obtained my PhD degree in August 2024 at the University of Minnesota (Advisor: Kai-Wen Lan). Before that, I was an undergraduate student at Fudan University.

I am interested in number theory and related topics. Currently, I am thinking about problems related to integral models of Shimura varieties and their compactifications.
